# Deescalation Explorer
It's 2026. Iran witnessed a massacre un early Jan, and now is in war with Israel and the US. While BB and Trump steer the war, the regime in Iran has shot the Internet down (again), now for more than a month, and unleashed its the propaganda machine instead. The population and the infrastructure are suffering from bomardments from Israel, Trump's numerous ultimatums, and the regime's open supression. Meanwhile, the Iranian diaspora are fragmented more than ever. Plenty of (wrong) dillemas are shattering even rooted friendships. Many are isolated. In their own sphere, of anger, distress, and perhaps, on top of all, hoplessness.

    
There are a couple of lingering questions that many of the Iranian diaspora (including me) ask themselves:
- What can I do as an indivdual?
- Shall I become a digital/social activist?
- Should I start a campaign? 
- Should I start an organization and internation lobbying?
- What is the range of my influence anyway?

I felt truly confused and lost. So, I used my avaiable resources (i.e., the Internet, which is a luxtury for the majority of Iranians now) to figure out what are feasible targets, possible strategies to reach them, and how can I (or we) implement them. 

This is the main goal of [this dashboard](https://arashgmn.github.io/deescalation-explorer/). To understand different strategies to act, their risks, and their timescale.

# Target audience
If you have a clarity on to which area you'd like to act, then you're good. Don't waste your time here. Otherwise, if you feel confused and frustrated, you may find the framework presented in this dashboard somewhat useful.
